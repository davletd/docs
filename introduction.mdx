## Welcome to Cloudgeni AI Agent

Cloudgeni AI Agent is a powerful tool designed to help you manage and monitor your infrastructure, identify issues, and ensure compliance with best practices. With Cloudgeni, you can:

- **Monitor Infrastructure**: Identify and fix infrastructure issues automatically
- **Ensure Compliance**: Track cloud security and compliance requirements
- **Optimize Costs**: Reduce cloud spending with intelligent recommendations
